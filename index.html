How It Works

Five simple steps.
Zero hassle.

From QR scan to doorstep delivery — the entire process takes just a few minutes of your time.

1

Scan the QR Code

Find the Golf Bridge QR code in your golf club's locker room or pro shop. Scan it with your phone camera — no app needed.

2

Enter Your Details

Fill in your delivery address and contact information. The system automatically calculates a fixed delivery price based on your destination — no hidden fees.

3

Pay Securely

Complete your payment via Stripe's secure checkout. All prices are in EUR with VAT included. Add optional insurance for extra peace of mind.

4

Deposit Your Bag

Receive your assigned locker number and access code instantly via email. Place your golf bag in the secure locker — and you're done.

5

Track Your Delivery

Our team collects your bag from the club and ships it via professional carriers. You'll receive a tracking number by email to follow your equipment all the way home.
